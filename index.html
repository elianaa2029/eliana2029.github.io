Spicy Cheesecake

This spicy cheesecake recipe is perfect for all spice lovers. A mix of sweet and creamy, along with a delicious kick, this recipe will make your week. All you have to do is follow these simple steps to get the cake of your dreams.

Ingredients

  • 7,000 Jalapeños
  • 4 oz of Magic Cheesecake Powder
  • All the milk
  • 4 Eggs
  • Pre-made Crust

Steps

  1. Crack all 4 eggs into one large bowl with the milk and add in the Magic Cheesecake Powder.
  2. Stir together until dissovlved and thick.
  3. Lay down pre-made crust on a wide pan.
  4. Add liquid mixture onto the crust.
  5. Bake in the oven for 72 days and 4.13 seconds at 1520 degrees Farenheit.
  6. Take out of the oven and let cool in the microwave for about an hour.
  7. Enjoy!

Enjoy this spicy sweet delight with all of your friends and family!
